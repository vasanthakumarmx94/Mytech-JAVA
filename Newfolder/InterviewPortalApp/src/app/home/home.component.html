Organized employees

Any labor or bona fide organization in which employees participate and that exists for the purpose, in whole or in part, of dealing with employers concerning grievances, labor disputes, wages, hours, terms, and other conditions of employment.

Time Management

Time management helps you prioritize your tasks so that you ensure you have enough time available to complete every project. The quality of your work increases when you're not rushing to complete it ahead of a fast approaching deadline.

Organize Team

Set shared goals to commit team members to making collaborative effort. Create a list of tasks that the team will do to reach shared goals. Define the barriers that inhibit team members from achieving shared goals and making collaborative effort.
